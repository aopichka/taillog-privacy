# Privacy Policy for TailLog
Last updated: March 1, 2026

TailLog ("we," "us," or "our") is a plane spotting logbook app. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect
Account information
When you create an account, we collect your email address. This is used solely for authentication.

Location
TailLog uses your device's GPS to show aircraft near you. When you log a spotting, your precise location at that moment is saved alongside the spotting record. We do not collect your location in the background.

Spotting records
When you log a spotting, we store details about the aircraft (registration, callsign, airline, type, altitude, speed) and the time and location of the sighting.

Photos
If you attach photos to a spotting, they are uploaded to and stored on our servers.

App preferences
We store a single preference: whether you prefer to use an external camera app.

What We Do Not Collect
We do not collect analytics or usage data
We do not track you across other apps or websites
We do not sell your data to anyone
We do not use advertising SDKs
How We Use Your Data
Your data is used exclusively to provide the app's features:

Displaying aircraft near your location
Building and displaying your personal spotting logbook
Backing up your spottings to your account
Data Storage
Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database and authentication service. Supabase stores data on servers in the United States. You can review Supabase's privacy practices at supabase.com/privacy.

No other third parties receive your data.

Your Rights
Access and export: You can view all your spotting data within the app at any time.

Deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from the Profile screen in the app. Deletion is immediate and irreversible. This removes your account, all spotting records, and all uploaded photos.

Children's Privacy
TailLog is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy
If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
